Who We Are



We are GlassCode Club, a design and development team. We design thoughtful digital experiences, build beautiful brand aesthetics. Everything from business-ready custom websites to rich interactive web apps.

Founded in 2018, since then till now, we have mastered the craft of web development and digital marketing, ensuring that the client businesses flourish and create a unique brand experience. We help enterprise businesses in their digital transition through our wide range of web/mobile development services. Our graphic designers and source code writers are always ready to work, making sure that we deliver exactly what you want for your business or an idea, whether it be a static website, an e-commerce website or even brochures.

Define

We aim to grasp the knowledge about the project when the clients reaches us. We take the time to research, understand your business and gather all the desired information to ensure that we and our clients are on the same page. Our team discusses your needs, requirements and expectations from the product to flesh out deliverables.

Design

Once we have enough information along with appropriate wireframes, we have thorough deliberations with the client about how we plan to approach the project. Our designers build the face of the product which will portray how the product will look like when it is finished. We refine the appearance of the elements and create a design to the customer satisfaction. After adding final touches, we proceed towards the development stage.

Build

We begin with coding process in various stages. Each stage set the pace for every milestone. It helps in making further plans more carefully. Our key to deliver successful results is to keep the processes simple and efficient. All our projects go through different layers of testing and quality assurance, to make sure that the final deliverable is perfectly working. Through this process in development we ensure exellence and help the client to get more clarity of the product.

Launch

The final product is delivered and assistance is offered to help with deployment. We create and execute a marketing plan to promote the product. Thanks to our considerable experienced team that saves time during the final stages of development.
